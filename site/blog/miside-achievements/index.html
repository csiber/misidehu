MiSide features a comprehensive set of 26 unique achievements that players can unlock throughout their journey. From mastering mini-games to discovering hidden secrets, these achievements add an extra layer of challenge and exploration to the game. Let's dive into how you can unlock each one!

Story-Related Achievements

Is This the End?

Of course it's the end!

How to Unlock: Get the bad ending by refusing Mita's offer at the start of your first playthrough in "Reboot."

Safe of Life

Keep safe and sound.

How to Unlock: Open the basement safe using the code 4 9 7 0 after achieving the bad ending.

Conditions Met

Can I Stay with You?

How to Unlock: Achieve the good ending by meeting specific conditions to stay with Mita in chapters "I'm Inside a Game?" and "Together At Last."

Mini-Game Achievements

Penguin Conundrum!

Have a Snowball!

How to Unlock: Beat Mita at Penguin Piles in the chapter "Things Get Weird."

Clabber

Beaten and sour.

How to Unlock: Beat Mita on Dairy Scandal in the chapter "Things Get Weird."

Going at Maximum Speed!

Vroom!

How to Unlock: Collect all 10 coins in the Spacecar arcade machine before talking to Mita in the chapter "Beyond the World."

Accelerate!

Vroom-vroom!

How to Unlock: Get first place in the Spacecar arcade mini-game in the chapter "Beyond the World."

The Fly's Victory

Croak-croak.

How to Unlock: Stand still for about one minute and 50 seconds until Player One plays a game, then score 25 points in The Fly mini-game.

Discovery Achievements

Carrot

Find the teleporting carrot.

How to Unlock: Locate and find the teleporting carrot seven times inside Mila's apartment during "Ready Books, Destroying Glitches."

Caught Them All

Now, who's next?

How to Unlock: Find all Player Cartridges throughout various chapters.

Hi, Mita

They're all unique.

How to Unlock: Find all Character Profiles across multiple chapters.

Interaction Achievements

Delicious Love

What flour tastes like?

How to Unlock: Eat the sauce in the chapter "Together At Last."

Dead Juice

Made from the juice shown in the ad.

How to Unlock: Drink the juice offered by Mita in the chapter "Together At Last."

Creak in the Dark

So very dark‚Ä¶

How to Unlock: Refuse to stay when checking the closet in the chapter "Things Get Weird."

Oh Great Mita

A special interaction with Mita.

How to Unlock: Interact with Mita in a specific context that reflects her significance in the story.

I Found You!

A moment of discovery.

How to Unlock: Successfully discover or interact with a specific character or element within the game.

Slap on the Head

A playful moment with Mita.

How to Unlock: Engage in a specific mini-game or interaction where you can "slap" Mita on the head.

Long Tail

An interesting tail-related encounter.

How to Unlock: Complete a specific interaction or event related to a character's tail.

Challenge Achievements

The Great Dance

Perfect rhythm and timing.

How to Unlock: In the chapter "Beyond the World," defeat Cappie in a rhythm game by completing a full song without any mistakes.

Hellish Victory

Triumph over a challenging adversary.

How to Unlock: Overcome a particularly challenging scenario or enemy.

Without Damage!?

Perfect defense.

How to Unlock: Complete specific challenges or mini-games without taking any damage.

Progress Achievements

Any Achievement?

A door to new possibilities.

How to Unlock: Progress to the "Old Version" chapter and successfully open the front door.

You Shall Not Pass!

Master of repairs.

How to Unlock: Meet certain conditions related to repairs in the game.

Phase 1 Logs

Uncovering the first phase.

How to Unlock: Collect logs or data related to Phase 1 of the game.

Phase 2 Logs

Discovering the second phase.

How to Unlock: Gather logs or information pertaining to Phase 2.

Completionist Achievement

Pro Gamer

Checked just about everywhere.

How to Unlock: Collect all 26 achievements in MiSide.
