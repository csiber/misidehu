A Quick MiSide Walkthrough

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

⚠️ Spoiler Warning

This walkthrough contains major spoilers for MiSide's story and gameplay. We recommend playing through the game first to experience it yourself.

Welcome to our quick walkthrough of MiSide! This guide will take you through all chapters of this psychological horror game, helping you navigate its mysteries and uncover its secrets.

Prologue

Your journey begins with the MiSide app. Over 37 days, you'll interact with Mita and perform daily tasks. Everything changes on day 37 when Mita expresses her desire to meet you face-to-face.

Chapter 1: I'M INSIDE A GAME?

Mita transports you into her game world and instructs you to put your phone down. The game's setting becomes remarkably realistic as you begin your quest to locate Mita.

Chapter 2: TOGETHER AT LAST

You finally meet Mita in person within the game world. Together, you'll explore the house and begin to uncover its secrets.

Chapter 3: THINGS GET WEIRD

The game world starts exhibiting strange and unsettling phenomena, causing you to question the reality of your surroundings.

Chapter 4: THE BASEMENT

You venture into the basement of the house, discovering hidden areas. The atmosphere becomes increasingly eerie as you delve deeper.

Chapter 5: BEYOND THE WORLD

You step beyond the known boundaries of the game world, encountering new challenges as reality and the virtual world continue to blur.

Chapter 6: CAP

You meet a version of Mita wearing a cap, introducing new dynamics to your interactions. This encounter adds complexity to your understanding of Mita.

Chapter 7: THE LOOP

You find yourself trapped in a repeating cycle of events. Breaking free requires careful observation and action.

Chapter 8: MINI MITA

A smaller version of Mita appears, presenting new puzzles and challenges. Interacting with Mini Mita offers deeper insights into the game's narrative.

Chapter 9: DUMMIES AND FORGOTTEN PUZZLES

You encounter mannequins and solve long-forgotten puzzles. These challenges reveal more about the game's backstory.

Chapter 10: GHOSTLY

Ghostly apparitions begin to haunt the game world. Confronting these entities is essential to progress further.

Chapter 11: SHE JUST WANTS TO SLEEP

Mita expresses her exhaustion and desire to rest. You must assist her in finding peace within the game.

Chapter 12: NOVELS

Reading in-game novels provides crucial insights into the overarching story. The lines between fiction and reality continue to blur.

Chapter 13: READING BOOKS, DESTROYING GLITCHES

By engaging with books, you can identify and eliminate glitches in the game. This process is vital for restoring the game's stability.

Chapter 14: RUN AND HIDE!

You are pursued by unknown threats, necessitating stealth and quick thinking. Survival depends on your ability to evade capture.

Chapter 15: OLD VERSION

Exploring an older version of the game reveals its history and development. This journey uncovers secrets about Mita's origins.

Chapter 16: OPEN DIALOG

Engaging in deep conversations with Mita unravels more of her backstory. Your emotional connection with her grows stronger.

Chapter 17: THE REAL WORLD?

Doubts about the nature of reality intensify. You must determine what is real and what is part of the game.

Chapter 18: REBOOT

Attempting a system reboot, you hope to fix the game's deeper issues. However, this action leads to unforeseen consequences.

💡 Gameplay Tips

  • Pay close attention to environmental details and dialogue - they often contain crucial hints.
  • Save your game frequently, especially before making important decisions.
  • Take your time to explore each area thoroughly - many secrets are hidden in plain sight.
  • Keep track of any codes or numbers you find - they might be useful later.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different choices to see how they affect the story.

This is just a quick walkthrough of MiSide's story and gameplay. For more detailed information, you can refer to the MiSide Wiki.
